Cultural Nite

Cultural Nite is the curtain raiser event of Aarohi. It is a charity event organized for people belonging to all age groups. Cultural Nite was started in the year 2005 and completed it's 11th edition this year. Some NGO's associated with Cultural Nite are Swikar, Ekal Vidyalaya, Shraddhanand Anathalaya, Child Adoption for Education (CAFE), Aamhi Aamcha Aarogyasathi, Deepstambh etc. Cultural Nite has supported these NGO's in their social cause. Artists like Amit Kumar-Sumit Kumar (Kishor Kumar's sons), Avadhoot Gupte, Vinod Rathod, Devaki Pandit, have mesmerised the audience in their previous editions. A marathi play 'ALL THE BEST' was also organised. Performance by renowned Marathi music composer, singer and author Saleel Kulkarni and famous Marathi playback singer Arya Ambekar was organised in Cultural Nite-2015.
