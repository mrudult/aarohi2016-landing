About us

Aarohi is the annual cultural festival of Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur held towards the end of February every year. Conceptualized way back in 1988, Aarohi has come a long way in all realms both qualitatively and quantitatively and has firmly ensonced itself as the centrepiece of the cultural spectrum of the region. It was started with the motive of promoting culture amongst students in Central India on a national platform. Today, it is the largest youth cultural festival held in Central India with a participation of around 5000 students & a whopping footfall of over 20,000. Aarohi, over the years has seen concerts like Sunburn Campus with Kash Trivedi, Coke Studio Night with The Raghu Dixit Project and performance by bands like Indigo Children, Workshop, Faridkot, Parikrama, MTV fame Agnee. It has also been graced by personalities like Urmila Matondkar, Avdhoot Gupte, Devaki Pandit, The marathi duo Dr. Saleel Kulkarni & Miss. Aryaa Ambekar. We are a Non-Profit Organisation completely organised and co-ordinated by a group of VNITians working together in the pursuit of a common cause, to produce meaningful results for the organisation.
